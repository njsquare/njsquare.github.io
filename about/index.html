About

Nipun Jaswal is an accomplished International Cyber Security Author and an award winning IT security researcher having a decade of experience in Red Teaming, Vulnerability Research, Reverse Engineering and Hardware Hacking.

He is Currently one of the Director at Sempersol Pvt. Ltd., Syfor Technologies Pvt. Ltd and serves as the CISO for Council of Information Security. He is a Software Engineer and Holds Masters of Technology(M.tech) Degree from LPU.

He has authored 8 English and 3 Chinese Books on Penetration Testing and Network Forensics. He is certified with C|EH (Certifed Ethical Hacker), C|ES (Certified Encryption Specialist), OSWP (Offensive Security Wireless Professional), OSCP (Offensive Security Certifed Expert), SLAE (SecurityTube Assembly Language Expert) certifications.

He has authored numerous exploits and zero days in various software ranging from Opera Browser to Application Server Software. Nipun represented India at BRICS Future Skills Challenge, 2018, SA where team india stood second among the five nations. Nipun also lead the team winning the first ever DSCI Hackathon in 2017. Nipun is a part of Cyber Coordination Center and NCDRC (National Cyber Defense and Research Center) as one of the chair members. Additionally, He is part of ALCS UK as well.
